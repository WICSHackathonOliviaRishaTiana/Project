About Fearless Footsteps!

We are Fearless Footsteps! A startup dedicated women, students, and any community that frequently feels endangered as a pedestrian. Fearless Footsteps believe that it is incredibly important to find the safest route possible based on traffic, crime rates, and time of day.

Our Data:

We see how women and students feel and take their concerns very seriously. We have used this data in order to add filters to our map searches to find the safest routes possible.

You Should Know:

Pedestrian deaths are in a 77% increase since 2010 and nighttime deaths have increased by 86%. 56% of women say that they feel unsafe in unlit urban areas while walking. 85% of girls have exerperienced harrassment as a pedestrian by the time they are 17. 74% of college students feel unsafe walking home in the dark.
